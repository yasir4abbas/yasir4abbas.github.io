Andromeda

Consumer & Business, Smart Home

Andromeda is a smart home platform with a goal of providing a single platform for smart devices to connect with. Allowing accessory makers and DIY enthusiasts to create products that would be compatible with a single provided platform to take advantage of already baked software functionalities. The software is capable of handling widely used connectivity protocols & provides Automation features that enhance the overall ecosystem experience.
